Your Day 1 guide. Follow this checklist to make sure you're fully set up and ready to start evaluating.

## Day 1 Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Add your credentials, specialties, and professional details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up payment information">
    Configure your payment method so you can get paid promptly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review onboarding document">
    Check the Contracts tab for your onboarding document if you have an active offer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify project tool access">
    Ensure you can access required tools like Slack, the evaluation platform, etc.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate contractor email">
    If a contractor email has been provisioned, activate it now.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review policies">
    Read the Policies section to understand AI usage and compliance guidelines.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigating the Portal

| Page | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Home** | Your dashboard with pending invitations, active tasks, and announcements |
| **Tasks** | View and manage your assigned evaluation tasks |
| **Explore** | Browse open opportunities you can apply to |
| **Earnings** | Track your completed tasks, payouts, and referrals |
| **Performance** | View your evaluation quality scores and metrics |
| **Profile** | Update your credentials, availability, and payment settings |

## Time Tracking

For hourly projects, you may be required to track your time using a designated tool. Guidelines:

- Only track active work hours
- Do not include breaks or idle time
- Review your daily logs for accuracy before submitting
- Log time on the same day you work when possible

<Tip>
  If you don't see your onboarding document or project tools within a few days of accepting an offer, reach out to your project contact or email support.
</Tip>
