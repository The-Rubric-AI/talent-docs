# Policies & Compliance

Review the policies that govern your work with Rubric AI. Compliance with these guidelines is required for all experts.

## AI & LLM Usage Policy

When completing evaluation tasks, your assessments must reflect your own professional judgment.

<Warning>
  Violations of AI usage, confidentiality, or time tracking policies may result in immediate removal from the platform and forfeiture of pending payouts.
</Warning>

### What's Not Allowed

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="No AI-Generated Responses" icon="robot">
    Do not use LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) to generate your evaluation responses or scores.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No External AI Tools" icon="ban">
    Do not copy-paste task content into external AI tools.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### What's Allowed

- Using reference materials, medical databases, and professional guidelines to inform your evaluation
- Looking up clinical information in trusted sources
- Using calculators or conversion tools

<Note>
  Automated or AI-generated submissions will be flagged and may result in removal from the platform.
</Note>

## Time Tracking & Pay

Accurate time tracking is essential for hourly projects:

- Only log hours for active, focused work on assigned tasks
- Do not log time for breaks, setup, or idle periods
- Review and confirm your daily time logs for accuracy
- Discrepancies in time tracking may be audited and adjusted

## Confidentiality & IP Protection

All project materials are confidential:

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Keep materials on approved platforms | Share, screenshot, or distribute materials |
| Follow project-specific guidelines | Discuss project details publicly |
| Report security concerns | Store materials on personal cloud drives |

## Earnings & Disputes

Compensation is based on completed and approved tasks.

If you believe a task was incorrectly rejected or a payout is missing:

1. Gather your task ID and relevant details
2. Contact support at [support@rubric.ai](mailto:support@rubric.ai)
3. Disputes are typically resolved within 5 business days
