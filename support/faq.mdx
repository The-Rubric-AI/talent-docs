## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I get matched with tasks?">
    Tasks are matched based on your profile â€” credentials, specialties, and availability. Keep your profile complete and up to date to receive the most relevant invitations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When will I get paid?">
    Payouts are processed bi-weekly. After a task is completed and approved, payment is included in the next payout cycle. You can track payout status on the Earnings page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I work on multiple projects at once?">
    Yes, you can be active on multiple projects simultaneously. Manage your availability in your Profile settings to ensure you can meet all deadlines.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I can't complete a task by the deadline?">
    Contact your project lead or support as soon as possible. Deadlines can sometimes be extended, but communication is critical.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is my performance measured?">
    Your performance is tracked through evaluation quality scores, consistency metrics, and completion rates. View detailed metrics on the Performance page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I found a bug in the platform. How do I report it?">
    Email support@rubric.ai with a description of the issue, steps to reproduce, and a screenshot if possible. Include your browser and operating system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I update my credentials or specialties?">
    Yes, you can update your profile at any time from the Profile page. Changes to credentials may require re-verification.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### When contacting support, include:

- Your account email
- Task ID (if applicable)
- A clear description of the issue
- Screenshots (if relevant)

## Emergency Issues

For urgent issues that affect your ability to work (platform outages, payment problems, etc.), include **URGENT** in your email subject line.
