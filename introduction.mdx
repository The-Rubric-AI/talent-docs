Thank you for joining the Rubric AI expert network. This documentation will help you navigate the platform, understand our processes, and get the most out of your experience.

## What is Rubric AI?

Rubric AI partners with healthcare organizations to evaluate and improve AI systems used in clinical settings. As an expert evaluator, you'll review AI-generated content and provide professional assessments that help ensure these systems meet the highest standards of quality and safety.
