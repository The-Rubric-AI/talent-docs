# Apply for a Task

Rubric AI matches you with evaluation tasks based on your expertise. Here's how to find and apply for tasks.

## Finding Tasks

Tasks appear in two places:

- **Home Dashboard** — Shows tasks you've been directly invited to based on your profile
- **Explore Page** — Browse all open opportunities and express interest

## Task Types

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Voice Evaluations" icon="microphone">
    Review and score AI-generated voice interactions with patients. You'll listen to audio recordings and assess accuracy, appropriateness, and clinical quality.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Chat Evaluations" icon="comments">
    Assess single-turn or multi-turn chat conversations between AI systems and users. Evaluate response quality, safety, and helpfulness.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Clinical Notes" icon="file-medical">
    Evaluate AI-generated clinical documentation including SOAP notes, discharge summaries, and other medical records.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Image / DICOM" icon="x-ray">
    Review AI analysis of medical images including radiology, pathology, and other diagnostic imaging.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Accepting a Task

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the task">
    Read the task description, requirements, and compensation details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept the task">
    Click Accept to add it to your active tasks queue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the work">
    Finish the task within the specified deadline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for review">
    Submit your evaluation for review and approval.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Once you accept a task, you are expected to complete it within the deadline. Repeatedly abandoning accepted tasks may affect your eligibility for future invitations.
</Warning>
