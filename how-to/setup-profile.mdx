A complete profile helps us match you with the most relevant evaluation tasks based on your expertise. Take time to fill out each section accurately.

## Profile Sections

### Personal Information

Provide your basic contact information and professional details:

- **Full legal name** — Must match your payment account and tax documents
- **Email** — Primary contact for task notifications
- **Phone number** — Required for contract offers (verified via SMS)
- **Location** — Country and timezone for scheduling

### Professional Background

Share your clinical or professional credentials:

- **Current role** — Your primary professional position
- **Years of experience** — Total years in your field
- **Employer/Organization** — Current or most recent workplace
- **Professional licenses** — State/country and license numbers

### Specialties & Skills

Select all areas where you have expertise:

- **Clinical specialties** — Medical specialties, subspecialties, or focus areas
- **Languages** — Languages you can evaluate content in
- **Technical skills** — Relevant software, systems, or methodologies

### Availability

Set your work preferences:

- **Hours per week** — How many hours you're available to work
- **Preferred schedule** — Days and times you're typically available
- **Start date** — When you can begin accepting tasks

<Tip>
  Keep your profile updated. Changes to your specialties or availability may affect which tasks you're invited to.
</Tip>

## Credential Verification

Some credentials may require verification before you can access certain task types. This typically involves:

- Uploading a copy of your professional license
- Providing your NPI number (for US healthcare providers)
- Completing a brief assessment

You'll be notified if additional verification is needed for tasks you apply to.
